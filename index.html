Our Donate Plan

We are donating those pepole who are recently lost there every thing in the disaster. We are distribute food, shelter and child education.

Food for a family

Safe food and water supplies are a major concern following a natural disaster. Our first step is giving food. Knowing how to safely handle food and water in the immediate few days after the disaster can reduce stress, worry and inconvenience.

Shelter for a family

Finding shelter is critical in times of disaster. Shelter outside of the hazard area could include staying with family or friends, seeking a hotel room, or staying in a mass shelter. We are donating shelter for those pepole.

Education for a child

Education is a fundamental right of all children, but children in emergencies are constantly denied this right - in 2015 humanitarian emergencies and protracted crises disrupted the education of more than 80 million children.
